VDX (Video-Driven-Experience) In-Browser App

2021 MAZDA CX-9 VDX

ROLE: DIGITAL DESIGN, UX.

I teamed up with Epsilon and Garage Team Mazda to create an in-browser app spotlighting Mazda vehicles. I took on the task of refining the mobile user experience.
My role specifically involved redesigning the VDX template, enhancing assets, crafting detailed redlines, and developing high-fidelity prototypes.

2021 Mazda CX-9 VDX – Mobile

VDX Mobile Wireframe Redlining

VDX Desktop

Prototype Flow View (Adobe XD)

Interactive Prototype (Adobe XD Embed)

IMPACT AND RESULTS

The redesigned in-browser app significantly enhanced user engagement, as evidenced by increased interactions and higher traffic to Mazda’s website.

This success was a clear indication of the effectiveness of the updated design, which resonated well with the target audience and contributed to an increase in brand and vehicle awareness.

TAKEAWAYS

Harmonizing Mazda's visual standards with UX design principles led to innovative problem-solving. Adhering to brand guidelines while integrating fresh UX strategies created an engaging user experience that stays true to the brand's identity.
