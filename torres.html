BRANDING MATERIAL

TORRES RIVER FRIENDS

ROLE: ART DIRECTION, ILLUSTRATION

I worked with Torres River Friends, a community-supported nonprofit focused on protecting the Torres River in San José, Costa Rica. Our goal was to design a mascot that would boost the organization's brand identity and connect with both loyal members and young enthusiasts.

My role was to create a distinctive mascot brand design. This included developing the character’s unique identity, illustrating it, and establishing a consistent style across all branding materials.

THE LOGO AND MASCOT EVOLUTION

The logo, featuring a charming character embracing a valuable water droplet, set the foundation and became a key visual in developing the brand mascot. For the mascot, we explored several design options, ultimately deciding to retain the smiley face and minimalistic style from the original logo.
